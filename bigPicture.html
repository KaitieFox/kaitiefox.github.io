What I Do

I am a native Clevelander with a passion for technology. As a recent graduate of We Can Code IT, a twelve week coding bootcamp in Cleveland, I have intense hands-on coding experience with several different coding languages:
C# .NET HTML CSS JavaScript.
The crowning achievement at We Can Code IT was the group project, where we had three weeks to complete a totally new web application. The start of the process was pitches for possible web applications. Of the 20 pitches, 4 were selected. My pitch was one of the four. From there we were assigned groups and brought the pitch to life in three weeks. It was incredibly rewarding to see the project from conception to actuality in that short amount of time.

Who I Am

Before deciding to pursue technology, I attended Malone University where I earned my BA with honors in Theological Studies. During college and in the years after I made it a point to travel as much as I could. I feel at home in many different places, countries, and cultures. When I returned to Cleveland in 2016 I began to send down roots. I got involved with local dance studio and reignited my passion for dance. Now, I teach ballroom dance at Viva Dance Studio and coach the Cleveland Dance Konnection performance team. I haven't lost my love for travel, but now I travel to perform with my team at festivals and dance events.
